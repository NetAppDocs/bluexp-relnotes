== Digital Advisor

=== 1 November 2022

Digital Advisor (formerly Active IQ) is now fully integrated with BlueXP and has an enhanced login experience. 

When you access Digital Advisor in BlueXP, you're now prompted for your NetApp Support Site credentials so that you can view data related to your systems. The NSS account that you log in with is associated with your user login only. It's not associated with any other users in your NetApp account.

For more details about Digital Advisor integration with BlueXP, go to the https://docs.netapp.com/us-en/active-iq/index.html[Digital Advisor documentation^]
